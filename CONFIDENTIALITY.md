## 🤝 Our Commitment

CommunityConnect AI is committed to protecting the confidentiality and privacy of all users, including:

- **Volunteers** who dedicate their time to social causes
- **NGOs** working to create positive change
- **Community beneficiaries** seeking assistance

As an AI-powered platform handling sensitive social welfare data, we adhere to strict data protection standards and best practices.

---

## 📊 Data Collection & Usage

### Principles

| Principle | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Minimal Collection** | Only collect data reasonably necessary for platform functionality |
| **Purpose Limitation** | Use data only for stated purposes |
| **Transparency** | Be clear about what data we collect and why |

### AI & Data Processing

- Matching algorithms process data solely to optimize volunteer deployments
- Personally Identifiable Information (PII) is **anonymized** before ML model training
- No user data is sold to third parties or used for external marketing

### Data We Collect

| Data Type | Purpose |
|-----------|---------|
| Contact information | Account creation, communication |
| Skills & availability | Volunteer-task matching |
| Location data | Proximity-based matching |
| Activity history | Performance tracking, impact measurement |

## 🛡️ Security Controls

### Encryption

| Type | Implementation |
|------|----------------|
| **In Transit** | TLS 1.3+ for all connections |
| **At Rest** | AES-256 encryption |

### Access Control

- **Role-Based Access Control (RBAC)** for all system access
- **Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)** for admin accounts
- Regular access audits and least-privilege enforcement

### Blockchain

- Volunteer impact hours are tokenized securely on Polygon network
- Underlying identity details are **never exposed** in blockchain transactions

## 👤 User Rights

### Your Data, Your Control

Every user retains full ownership of their personal data. You have the right to:

| Right | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Access** | Request a full copy of your collected data |
| **Opt-Out** | Disable AI data processing features |
| **Deletion** | Request immediate account and data deletion |
| **Portability** | Export your data in machine-readable format |

### How to Exercise Your Rights

1. **Email**: privacy@communityconnect.ai
2. **Subject Line**: Include "Data Request" or "Deletion Request"
3. **Response Time**: Within 30 days
